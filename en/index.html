How it works ↓

You don't know what your phone is listening to.

Apps access your mic without telling you.

Android shows a tiny dot. That's it.

No history. No alerts. No control.

Sovara tells you.

Real-time alerts

Sound, vibration, push notification, or LED flash — your choice. 8 alert sounds and 5 vibration patterns.

Knows which app

Identifies the calling app and the foreground app at the time of access. Not just a dot.

Works in standby

Detection continues when the screen is off. Your phone is guarded 24/7, not only when you're looking.

Exportable history

Full access log, unlimited duration, exportable to CSV or JSON. Filter by sensor, period, or duration.
